Our Learning Path

A1 Level: Foundations

Building basic AI literacy and fundamental English communication skills.

  • 16 Themes: AI Basics, Prompting, Job Search, Interviews, Business Ideas, Health Info, Finance, Learning, Creativity, Digital Well-being, Future Work, Community, Ethics, Content Creation, Language Learning, AI Limitations
  • 32 Lessons: 2 lessons per theme focusing on vocabulary, grammar, and basic AI interaction
  • Focus: Basic commands, simple questions, and foundational AI understanding
  • Duration: 8-12 weeks
  • Outcome: CEFR A1 Certification

A2 Level: Everyday Application

Applying AI tools to practical daily situations and improving conversational English.

  • 16 Themes: Same 16 topics with increased complexity
  • 32 Lessons: Intermediate applications and more detailed interactions
  • Focus: Follow-up questions, checking understanding, and practical AI usage
  • Duration: 8-12 weeks
  • Outcome: CEFR A2 Certification

  • 16 Themes: Advanced applications across all topics
